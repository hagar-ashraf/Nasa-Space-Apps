Earth savers

Our goal is to save the earth from a very dangerous event which can cause damages in satellites, communications, and TV, and cause electrical blackouts over whole continents.



Being in probable danger with a high serious risks makes us think of new solutions , and that’s exactly what we’re doing here. With the usage of Artificial Intelligence(AI) and Machine Learning(ML) we are dealing the Carrington Event problem with implementing a machine learning model for the DSCOVR spacecraft's FC instrument to track and follow the changes in the peak solar wind speed so that DSCOVR can continue to provide early warnings of the next potential Carrington-like event that could adversely impact life and property on Earth.
