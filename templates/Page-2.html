carrangiton event will have a lot of bad impacts on Earth and people. So, we provide for them some guidence to survive along thesw circuments.

Systematic guidence in dealing with Carrangiton event

Off the Grid Power

Buy a generator and extra fuel, or install a backup energy supply such as solar panels or a wind turbine.

Battery Backup for Computers

An Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS) looks a lot like a standard surge protector but contains batteries that keep computers running smoothly without damage during power fluctuations and brownouts.

Emergency Supplies

Create an emergency box with flashlights, batteries, cooking and heating fuel, food, and clean water. Also, consider a backup stash with paper copies of financial and personal records, cash, road maps, address book, radio, first-aid kit, and anything else you’d need.

Unplug Electronic Devices

Simply unplugging electronic devices will also ensure that they aren’t zapped by a power surge.
